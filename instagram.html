BeomaBot — Instagram Insights via Telegram

BeomaBot is a Telegram bot designed for creators and businesses who want to track performance of their Instagram content in a simple and secure way.

After logging in with Instagram, users can view summary statistics about their posts, reels, and profile directly in the bot. The data is retrieved via the Instagram Graph API and shown only to the account owner.

We do not share or resell any user data. Access is secure and available only to authenticated users.
