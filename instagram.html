Beoma – Instagram Analytics Telegram Bot

Beoma is a Telegram-based analytics service that allows Instagram Business and Creator accounts to receive daily performance reports of their content directly in Telegram.

What does Beoma do?

  • Provides an interface for Instagram account owners to authenticate using OAuth.
  • Collects media performance statistics via Instagram Graph API.
  • Sends summarized insights about Reels and other posts to the authorized user through a Telegram bot.

Who is this for?

Beoma is designed for content creators, influencers, and small businesses who want a lightweight, daily overview of their Instagram performance without using heavy analytics dashboards.

Data Privacy

Beoma only accesses and processes data for the account that authorized the bot. All data is used strictly for showing insights to the authenticated user. We do not store or share any personal data with third parties.
